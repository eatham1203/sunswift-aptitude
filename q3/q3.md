# Question 3
## Infotainment System Design & Safety Considerations
##### Expected time: ~30 minutes

### Background
An infotainment system in a race car is not just a display, it is a safety critical interface between the driver and the engineering team.  

A poorly designed system can confuse the driver, hide important faults, or even cause dangerous distractions at race speeds.

At Sunswift, our infotainment system must deliver real time information, handle unreliable telemetry, and remain responsive, even under extreme vibration, temperature, and network conditions.

 **2. Identify and explain _four_ key principles of good infotainment system design.**
Your chosen principles may include (but are not limited to):
- Fail-safe behaviour
- Predictability
- Minimal driver distraction
- Prioritisation of critical information

- Logging and fault detection
